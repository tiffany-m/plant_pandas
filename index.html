Genki (元気) is a Japanese word without a direct English translation. It roughly means having energy, pep, or health. We call ourselves Genki Panda because we believe our plant pandas will bring you and your family much luck and health!
When it comes to welcoming pets into your family, every pet needs to be cared for and loved. However, when you're a busy person, it's just not possible to spend every waking moment looking after a pet.
Plant pandas are not only low maintenance, but they help you take care of you home! All you need are some house plants. (Just don't forget to water them!) The pandas will take care of your plants as far as keeping them clean, trimmed (food!) and bug free (food!). Pandas will spend most of their days hanging out by the plants but also love to play games and catch bugs.

FAQ
Please read over these Frequently Asked Questions before calling. If you still need help feel free to call or message us. If we don't answer we will get back to you within 24 business day hours.

Do I need specific plants for the pandas to eat?

Yes and no! You don't need specific plants but you do need a variety. The more colors the better! Just like humans and how we eat fruits and vegetables, different color flowers provide different nutrients. You don't need all the plants to be in the same spot either. Placing them in different rooms actually promotes exercise for the pandas.

Do plant pandas need to be trained?

All plant pandas adopted from Genki Panda come pre-trained!

I have a cat/dog/other. Will the pandas get along?

NO - Larger animals such as cats and dogs tend to see the pandas as toys.
Yes - Pandas will get along with smaller pets such as bunnies and gerbils.
WARNING - Ant farms are suggested as not being within reach of pandas.

How big do they get? How long do they live?

Fortunately what you see is what you get for their size. From head to tail they grow to about 3.5" and weight 2.5oz. As long as they are taken care of they can live anywhere between 15 and 20 years.
