When it comes to welcoming pets into your family, every pet needs to be cared for and loved. However, when you're a busy person, it's just not possible to spend every waking moment looking after a pet.

Plant pandas are not only low maintenance, but they help you take care of you home! All you need are some house plants. (Just don't forget to water your plants!) Plant pandas will take care of your plants as far as keeping them clean, trimmed (food!) and bug free (also food!). Plant pandas will spend most of their days hanging out by the plants but also love to play games and catch bugs.
